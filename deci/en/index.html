Description

  • Each time randomly comes up heads, tails or edge.
  • Share last result of the flip or all stats at once.
  • With each flip the coin is levelling, level-ups open new coin skins.
  • Each side of the coin has a unique sound and vibration; results can be read without looking at the screen.
  • Turn on the auto-flip mode and see how the app is flipping (levelling) the coin for you.
  • Flip can be done with a tap, shake or by turning on auto-flip mode.

Development

This is my first Android app, made for educational purposes:

  • Single-Activity architecture.
  • SharedPreferences repository.
  • In-app purchases Google Play Billing Library.
  • BottomNavigationView, Fragments, WebView.
  • Firebase Crashlytics and Performance, Google Analytics.
  • Work with sound, animation and vibration.
  • Accelerometer for device shaking detection.
  • Day and night theme, the landscape mode.
  • Localization into English and Russian.
  • VCS: Git, GitHub.
  • Latest AndroidX components.
  • Material design according to the Google guidelines.
  • Full cycle development, publishing to the Google Play.
  • Support for older devices starting from Android 4.1 Jelly Bean (API 16).
  • Java, without using third-party libraries.
